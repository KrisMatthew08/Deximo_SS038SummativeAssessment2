II. Biological Factors

A. Inherited Traits

Rizal inherited intelligence and artistry from both sides of his family. His mother, Teodora Alonso, was known for her sharp mind and education.

Despite his small stature and poor eyesight, Rizal developed resilience and a strong self-discipline, proving that character can surpass physical limitations.

III. Environmental Factors

Rizal was raised in a well-off, principled family in Calamba. His brother Paciano and mother greatly influenced his moral and intellectual development.
